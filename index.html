About Us

Our Story

Founded by three passionate entrepreneurs who met at Harvard University, Leaf Us Café began its journey in London's Bond Street in 2000. Inspired by their mentor Miss Athirah, we've grown from a single location to over 100 stores across Asia, bringing our unique blend of coffee culture and sustainable practices to coffee lovers everywhere.
